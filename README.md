When the app opens, a selection menu appears. To start a new book, select the Novel template. This template organizes your files into sections like Manuscript, Characters, and Research. ### Interface Basics
The interface consists of three main parts. On the left, the Binder displays your chapters and research notes. The middle section functions as your writing area. On the right, the Inspector pane holds your project synopsis and notes. You can toggle these sections on or off from the View menu.

## 📝 Writing and Organizing
Use the Binder to move chapters around by dragging them with your mouse. If you want to view two chapters at once, use the split screen button in the top right corner. ## 💾 Saving Your Work
The software saves files automatically as you type. You can also press Ctrl and S on your keyboard to trigger a manual save. The application stores your project as a folder containing your document files. Do not move or rename files inside the Scrivener project folder via Windows Explorer, as this can break your project links.
